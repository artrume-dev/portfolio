5Audience Segments
Canon UK operates across five distinct audiences: consumer photographers, videographers, B2B enterprise print, production printing, and a growing digital services layer. Their product range spans entry-level cameras through to industrial inkjet presses — a massive, complex portfolio where AI has outsized potential to reduce friction, guide decisions, and unlock revenue that currently leaks to competitors. Canon has already proven product selectors can drive a 14% revenue increase and 53% conversion uplift — AI chat takes this dramatically further.

Marketing site and ecommerce are disconnected journeys — brand vs. buy friction
Gear locked behind retail glass with no digital expert substitute
B2B buyers can't self-serve complex MFP / production press comparisons
Post-purchase: no ongoing creative education to drive accessories & loyalty
